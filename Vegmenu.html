Szechuan noodles

Experience a burst of flavor with our Szechuan noodles! These perfectly cooked noodles are tossed in a spicy, savory sauce that features a blend of Szechuan peppercorns and garlic. Each bite offers a delightful kick, complemented by fresh vegetables for added crunch. Garnished with scallions and sesame seeds, this dish is as visually appealing as it is delicious. Ideal for spice lovers, it’s a vibrant meal that transports you straight to the streets of Sichuan. Dive into a bowl of these irresistible noodles and savor the heat!

Spicy Vegetable Pakoras

Spicy Vegetable Pakoras are a delightful Indian snack that combines a variety of fresh vegetables, such as potatoes, spinach, and cauliflower, coated in a spiced chickpea flour batter. Fried until golden and crispy, these fritters deliver a satisfying crunch with every bite. Served hot with tangy tamarind chutney or mint sauce, they make the perfect appetizer or tea-time treat.

Cauliflower chili

Begin by battering and frying cauliflower chilli until they’re crispy and golden. In a separate pan, garlic, ginger, and bell peppers, then add a tangy chili sauce for that perfect kick. Toss the fried cauliflower in the sauce until evenly coated, then garnish with fresh cilantro and a sprinkle of sesame seeds. This dish is not only a crowd-pleaser but also a fantastic vegetarian option for any meal.
